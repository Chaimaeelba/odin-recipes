Pasta Salad

Description

Pasta salad is a colorful and versatile dish made with cooked pasta, fresh vegetables, and a tangy dressing. Often including ingredients like cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and olives, it can be enhanced with proteins like chicken or feta cheese. Perfect for picnics or as a side dish, pasta salad is both refreshing and easy to customize.

Often topped with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or a sprinkle of powdered sugar, these pancakes offer a comforting and satisfying way to start the day. Perfect for using up overripe bananas, banana pancakes are a simple yet indulgent dish that can be enjoyed by all ages.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound tri-colored spiral pasta
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 6 tablespoons salad seasoning mix
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • Â½ yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives, chopped

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.




  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 10 to 12 minutes; rinse under cold water and drain.


  3. Whisk Italian dressing and salad spice mix together until smooth. Combine pasta, tomatoes, bell peppers, and olives in a salad bowl.




  4. Pour dressing over salad and toss to coat.




  5. For the best flavor, refrigerate the pasta salad for 8 hours to overnight.




  6. Enjoy!
