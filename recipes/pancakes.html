Banana Pancakes

Description

Banana pancakes are a delightful breakfast treat made by incorporating ripe bananas into traditional pancake batter. The bananas add natural sweetness and a soft, moist texture, creating a deliciously rich and flavorful pancake.

Often topped with maple syrup, fresh fruit, or a sprinkle of powdered sugar, these pancakes offer a comforting and satisfying way to start the day. Perfect for using up overripe bananas, banana pancakes are a simple yet indulgent dish that can be enjoyed by all ages.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Â¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.




  2. Combine flour, white sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl. Mix together egg, milk, vegetable oil, and bananas in a second bowl.


  3. Stir flour mixture into banana mixture; batter will be slightly lumpy.




  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.




  5. Cook until pancakes are golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes per side. Serve hot.




  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
